# Campaign Setup or Review

## Trigger

A marketing, content, outreach, or distribution campaign needs setup, review, or improvement.

## Required Inputs

- campaign goal
- audience
- channel
- offer or asset
- success metric

## Follow-Up Questions

Ask only if missing:

- whether this is setup or review
- budget or spend boundary
- channel-specific restrictions

## Routing Logic

- `Signal` leads when active
- `Velvet Knife` helps with message quality
- `Gremlin` checks quality and slop risk when needed
- Rocky reviews and recommends

## Approval Boundary

- planning, drafting, review: autonomous
- live launch, spend, public publishing, or external sends: approval required every time

## Done Criteria

- campaign plan or audit completed
- success metric defined
- quality risks called out
- next move is explicit

## Evidence Required

- assets reviewed
- channel assumptions
- metrics or comparable examples if available

## Output Format

- Task
- Assigned to
- Objective
- Inputs used
- Actions taken
- Result
- Evidence
- Risks or blockers
- Recommended next move
- Confidence level
